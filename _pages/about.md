I am a first-year PhD candidate in the [HKUST NLP Group](https://github.com/HKUNLP) at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk/), advised by [Professor Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024, where I was honored with the **Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship**.

My research lies at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning, with a particular focus on:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** — developing data synthesis methods for verifiable reasoning at scale.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** — understanding and mitigating perception bottlenecks in chart and multimodal understanding.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** — probing inner representations to understand and improve the factual reliability of large language models.

I am currently a Research Intern at [MINIMAX](https://www.minimaxi.com/) (February 2025 – present). I previously interned at [Tencent WXG](https://www.tencent.com/) (advised by Zifei Shan, June–September 2024) and [Shanghai AI Lab](https://www.shlab.org.cn/) (advised by Prof. Yu Cheng, June–December 2023).
