We systematically study the **perception bottleneck** in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) applied to chart understanding. Through controlled experiments, we show that VLM failures on charts stem primarily from inadequate low-level visual perception rather than reasoning deficiencies. We introduce the Vision4Chart benchmark and propose approaches to address visual perception limitations.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He
