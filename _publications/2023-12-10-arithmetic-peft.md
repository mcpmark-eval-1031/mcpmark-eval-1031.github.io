We investigate the composition of **parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) modules** — such as LoRA adapters — through arithmetic operations in the weight-difference space. By adding, subtracting, and scaling these compact modules, we achieve strong multi-task generalization without any additional training, demonstrating that PEFT modules encode transferable task-specific knowledge that can be algebraically combined.

**Authors:** Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He
