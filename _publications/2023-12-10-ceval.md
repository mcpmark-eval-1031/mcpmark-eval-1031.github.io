**C-Eval** is a large-scale Chinese evaluation benchmark spanning 52 academic disciplines and four difficulty levels (middle school, high school, college, and professional). It systematically measures the reasoning, knowledge, and comprehension abilities of foundation models in the Chinese-language setting and has been widely adopted to benchmark leading LLMs.

**Authors:** Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He
