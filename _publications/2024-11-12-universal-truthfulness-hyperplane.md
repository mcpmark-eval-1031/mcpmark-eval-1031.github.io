We probe the hidden-state space of large language models and identify a **universal truthfulness hyperplane** — a linear classifier that robustly separates true statements from false ones across diverse factual domains. The hyperplane transfers across LLM families and can improve factuality at inference time without retraining.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
