We introduce **SynLogic**, a scalable data-synthesis framework that generates large amounts of verifiable logical reasoning problems and uses reinforcement learning (RL) with correctness-based reward signals to train LLMs. SynLogic covers a diverse range of logical reasoning task types and yields substantial improvements on challenging benchmarks including mathematics, code, and multi-step deduction.

**Authors:** Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He
